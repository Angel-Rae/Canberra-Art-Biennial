City Commissions

is one of seven initiatives that artsACT is delivering as part of the Creative Recovery and Resilience Program.

artsACT is delivering the project to further its goals for recovery and resilience of the local arts sector following disruption due to the ongoing pandemic. The Creative Recovery and Resilience Program aims to pilot new models of delivering arts industry support; and to identify ongoing and emergent needs of the arts sector. contour 556 is delivering the City Commissions project on behalf of artsACT.

The City Commissions project tasks three artists with exploring the identity of the city as it is expressed through the contribution of community organisations. The artists will experience new collaborative opportunities, working closely with contour 556, artsACT and the community organisations to develop/install/perform/deliver their artwork.

Hit Print!
Alison Alder x Hands On Studio

Alison will work with Hands On Studio artsts. the project will raise awareness about the effects of social isolation and
extra difficulties. The project will create 3 text based prints and banners 8 x 2m with live spoken performances by eight artists.

Reticulum
Michael Sollis x Cancer Council ACT

Michael will weave together interviews from immunocompromised families with music inspired by the increasing isolation
of the stars. Working with Capital Region Cancer Relief. Michael will produce an object that will play interviews and sound.

Portrait
Sammy Hawker x Headspace Canberra

Sammy will work with participants from Headspace Canberra. She will take portraits, then the subjects will manipulate the
negative to add elements of their lockdown lives. The portraits will be displayed at multiple venues around Canberra.
